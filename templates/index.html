Why "Planckeon"?

Our name comes from planckeons—theoretical entities at the Planck scale (~10-35 m) where quantum mechanics and gravity merge. Recent research models them as the fundamental "grains" of spacetime, realizing the ER=EPR conjecture through networks of quantum wormholes.

Just as planckeons bridge the quantum and gravitational realms, we build software that bridges theoretical physics and practical computation.

Our Philosophy

Speed without intelligence is noise.
Intelligence without speed is impractical.
We build tools that are both.

Theoretical physics demands software that can handle symbolic manipulation at scale, numerical precision at the limits of floating-point, algorithmic sophistication for NP-hard problems, and intuitive interfaces for domain experts.

We don't believe researchers should choose between power and usability.
